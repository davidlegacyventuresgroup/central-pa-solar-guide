## Qualification FAQ

### How might a homeowner not qualify?

A homeowner may not qualify if:

- the roof is in poor condition and cannot support solar unless a roof replacement program applies
- the home has excessive shading or poor sun exposure
- financing or credit requirements are not met for certain $0 down programs
- the property is not owner-occupied

### What kind of roof warranty is included?

Depending on the project, homeowners may receive:

- roof penetration warranty protection
- workmanship warranties
- manufacturer warranties
- warranty coverage tied to a new roof if they qualify for roof replacement through the program
